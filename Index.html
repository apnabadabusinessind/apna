About Us

Our journey, mission, and the team behind your success

Our Story

Founded in 2015, ApnaBadaBusinessInd began with a simple mission: to help small and medium businesses overcome growth challenges. Our founder, Rajesh Sharma, noticed that many promising businesses struggled not because of their products, but due to lack of strategic direction.

Today, we've grown into a team of 25+ experts who have helped over 500 businesses achieve sustainable growth. Our approach combines data-driven strategies with practical implementation guidance.

Our Vision

To become the catalyst for 10,000 successful businesses by 2030, creating economic opportunities across India.

Our Mission

Empower entrepreneurs with actionable strategies, practical knowledge, and continuous support to build scalable businesses.

Core Values

Integrity, Excellence, Innovation, Collaboration, and Client Success drive everything we do.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to see results from your business growth strategies?
The timeline for seeing results varies based on your business size, industry, and the specific strategies implemented. Most clients start seeing measurable improvements within 3-6 months. We focus on both short-term wins and sustainable long-term growth.
What makes your approach different from other business consultants?
Our approach combines three key elements: 1) Deep industry-specific knowledge, 2) Practical implementation support beyond just strategy, and 3) Customized solutions rather than one-size-fits-all templates. We become true partners in your growth journey.
Do you work with startups or only established businesses?
We work with businesses at all stages - from pre-revenue startups to established companies looking to scale. About 40% of our clients are startups, 40% are small to medium businesses, and 20% are larger enterprises seeking transformation.
What industries do you specialize in?
We have expertise across multiple sectors including technology, manufacturing, retail, healthcare, education, and professional services. Our team includes specialists with deep experience in specific industries to provide relevant insights.
How are your fees structured?
We offer flexible engagement models: 1) Project-based pricing for specific initiatives, 2) Retainer model for ongoing support, and 3) Performance-based fees tied to achieving specific growth milestones. During our initial consultation, we'll recommend the best model for your needs.
What if I need help with implementation, not just strategy?
Implementation support is built into all our engagements. Unlike traditional consultants who just deliver reports, we provide hands-on assistance with execution, including training your team, setting up systems, and providing ongoing guidance.
